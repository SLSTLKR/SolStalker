SolStalker Token (SLSR) Whitepaper

Introduction

SolStalker Token (SLSR) is a decentralized cryptocurrency launched on the Solana blockchain. It is designed for fair distribution, market-driven pricing, transparency, and community development.

Blockchain and Token Details

  • Blockchain: Solana
  • Ticker: SLSR
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 SLSR
  • Launch Platform: Pump.fun
  • Future Listing: Raydium
  • Smart Contract: Fully auditable and immutable

Tokenomics

  • Liquidity Pool (Pump.fun): 100% (1,000,000,000 SLSR)
  • Fair Launch: No pre-mine or team allocation
  • Trading Mechanics: Entirely driven by supply and demand
  • Fixed Supply: No additional minting
  • Future Liquidity Transfer: Transition to Raydium for better accessibility and liquidity

Roadmap

Q1 2025 – Launch and Basic Stages

  • Token launch on Pump.fun with full liquidity provision.
  • Ensuring all tokens are placed in the Pump.fun liquidity pool for fair distribution.
  • Marketing campaign initiation.
  • Establishing official social media channels (Twitter, Telegram, Discord).
  • Attracting early investors and users.

Q2 2025 – Expansion and Exchange Listing

  • Listing on Raydium and liquidity migration.
  • Community growth through giveaways, contests, and educational initiatives.

Q3 2025 – Increased Liquidity and Partnerships

  • Developing strategic partnerships with other crypto projects.
  • Listing on major decentralized exchanges.
  • Introducing staking rewards and incentive programs.

Q4 2025 – Sustainable Growth

  • Expanding the ecosystem and integrating with DeFi applications.
  • Enhancing liquidity through additional pools.
  • Gathering community feedback to refine the tokenomics and development strategy.

Conclusion

SolStalker (SLSR) is a community-driven token with a fair launch, strong liquidity foundation, and a transparent development plan. By leveraging the power of the Solana blockchain and strategic ecosystem growth, SLSR aims to become a key player in the decentralized economy.
